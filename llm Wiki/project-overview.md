# Farmer Supply Chain - Project Overview

## Vision
The Farmer Supply Chain application is an end-to-end marketplace and traceability platform that connects agricultural producers, intermediaries, and industrial buyers. It aims to streamline trade, reduce transaction overheads, ensure transparency, and offer full farm-to-fork batch traceability.

## Core Entities
1.  **Farmer**: The raw producer (crops, vegetables, fruits).
2.  **Middleman (Commission Agent / Broker)**: Aggregates small volumes from unregistered farmers and connects them with commercial entities.
3.  **Trader (Wholesaler)**: Registered commercial entity that purchases, stores, grades, and sells agricultural products using standard invoices (GST).
4.  **Processing Factory**: Primary/secondary processing unit converting raw materials into processed ingredients (e.g., oil, flour).
5.  **By-Product Manufacturer**: Secondary/tertiary unit creating finished goods (e.g., chips, biscuits).
6.  **FMCG / Exporters / Bulk Consumers**: Industrial, international, or high-volume commercial buyers.
7.  **Transporters & Quality Assessors** *(Supporting)*: Handles transit logistics and grading validation.

## Target Audience & Value Proposition
*   **For Farmers**: Direct access to larger markets, transparent pricing, and less reliance on unfavorable middleman terms.
*   **For Traders & Factories**: Access to aggregated listings, verified batch quality, and simplified invoicing.
*   **For FMCG & Exporters**: Complete batch traceability, organic authenticity tracking, and consistent food safety compliance.
